Mountain Bikes

As mentioned before, mountain bikes are mainly used for biking in the wilderness. They can double down and be used on the road but this is not reccommended as the tires are soft and can do unexpected things on the road that may lead to an accident. They also wear much faster on the pavement than on the trails. Mountain biking can also be a few hours long or can become a journey known as "bike packing." Both activies are a lot of fun for people of all ages.

Road Bikes

As mentioned before, road bikes are very similar to each other. They all have similar styles to make the rider as aerodynamic as possible as well as have low gear ratios to allow the bike to go super fast. The main things that change within this world is tires and the material used to create the bike. Although these 2 items may seem insignificant, they can change the characteristics of the bike to make it feel as though it were a different bike! This activity can be done alone although it is usually done as a group.
